The Vision

A trusted network for everyday investors.

Public investing social apps are noisy. SignalCircle starts with trust: family, friends, small groups, and clear reasoning. Every signal should answer why, entry, target, risk, and time horizon.

Profiles: each user has a watchlist, track record, and investing style.
Private groups: family circle, options circle, long-term circle, AI stocks circle.
Ticker rooms: every stock has a shared discussion page and circle sentiment.
Alerts: notify the circle when a friend posts, price crosses 50D, or a target hits.
