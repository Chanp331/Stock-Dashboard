# SignalCircle

SignalCircle is a private investing circle prototype: family and trusted friends can build watchlists, share stock signals, explain why they like or avoid a ticker, and learn together.

## Product Direction

SignalCircle is bigger than one dashboard. The long-term vision is a trusted investing social network with:

- Personal top 50 watchlists
- Private family and friend circles
- Buy / hold / sell signal posts
- Entry, target, stop, confidence, and time horizon
- Ticker rooms for discussion around each stock
- AI daily digest of what the circle is watching
- Alerts for friend signals, price levels, 50D crosses, and target hits
- Profiles with investing style and track record

## Prototype Pages

- `index.html` - SignalCircle landing page
- `login.html` - prototype login/onboarding page
- `social-dashboard.html` - working watchlist and signal feed prototype

## Current Prototype Features

- Top watchlist dashboard
- Browser-side live quote refresh
- Buy / hold / sell signal cards
- Family and friends signal feed
- Post-a-signal form with entry, target, and reason
- Group pulse summary
- Local browser saving for watchlist and posts

## Notes

- The dashboard is currently a front-end prototype.
- Manual watchlist additions and signal posts are saved in each browser.
- Real accounts, shared group data, comments, and notifications require a backend database.
- This prototype is for education and discussion only, not financial advice.
